RESEARCH INTEREST

I am interested at planetary surface sediment-transport system and its respondance against environmental boundary conditions. Currently, I am looking at the evolution of submarine fan systems, using flume experiments, numerical simulations and facies analyses based on field survey of ancient rock record.
